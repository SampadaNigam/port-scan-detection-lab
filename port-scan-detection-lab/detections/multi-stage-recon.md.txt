# Multi-Stage Reconnaissance Detection

## 🎯 Objective
Detect sequential reconnaissance behavior such as:
- Host discovery
- Port scanning
- Service probing

## 🔍 Detection Logic (SPL)

index=* sourcetype=syslog OR sourcetype=kern.log "UFW BLOCK"
| rex "SRC=(?<src>\S+)"
| rex "DPT=(?<dpt>\d+)"
| eval scan_type=case(
    dpt=22, "SSH",
    dpt=53, "DNS",
    dpt=80 OR dpt=443, "Web",
    dpt<1024, "Privileged",
    true(), "General"
)
| stats count dc(dpt) as unique_ports values(scan_type) as scan_stages by src
| where unique_ports > 15 AND mvcount(scan_stages) > 2
| sort - unique_ports


## 🧠 Analyst Notes
- Multi-stage recon is more malicious than a single scan.
- Correlation across time windows increases detection accuracy.
