# High-Velocity Scan Detection

## 🎯 Objective
Identify rapid, high-volume connection attempts from a single source IP.

## 🔍 Detection Logic (SPL)

index=* sourcetype=syslog OR sourcetype=kern.log "UFW BLOCK"
| rex "SRC=(?<src>\S+)"
| bin _time span=1m
| stats count by src, _time
| where count > 50
| sort - count


## 🧠 Analyst Notes
- Useful for detecting aggressive Nmap scans.
- Thresholds can be tuned based on environment baseline.
