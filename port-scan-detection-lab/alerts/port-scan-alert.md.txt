# Port Scan Alert

## 🎯 Alert Purpose
Trigger when a source IP exceeds a defined threshold of blocked connection attempts.

## ⚙️ Alert Logic (SPL)

index=* ("UFW BLOCK")
| rex "SRC=(?<src>\S+)"
| rex "DPT=(?<dpt>\d+)"
| stats count dc(dpt) as unique_ports values(dpt) as ports by src
| where unique_ports > 10


## 🔔 Alert Settings
- Severity: Medium
- Trigger: Once per result
- Throttle: 30 minutes per source IP

## 🧠 Analyst Notes
- Helps identify active reconnaissance.
- Tune thresholds to reduce false positives.
